An stable foundation for a better Internet

Themelio is a new kind of public blockchain designed as a radically immutable trust substrate, powering unparalleled stability, security, and performance for the next generation of decentralized apps.

A blockchain for the long run

Unlike centralized services, blockchain protocols can’t really be updated.

“Protocol governance” is really about getting a whole community to collectively switch to a different protocol. And as with most collective action, contention, drama, and politics rule the day.

Instead, Themelio is designed not to change. That means a low-level, robust protocol focusing on doing one job well: providing an immutable root of trust, security, and transparency to a diverse ecosystem..

Uncompromising stability

Consensus-breaking updates, like “hard forks” and “protocol upgrades”, weaken the very basis of blockchains’ decentralized, trustless security and destabilize whole ecosystems. The unprecedented revolution in trust promised by blockchains can only be unlocked with a robust, stable protocol carefully engineered to not need change.

Themelio’s incredibly simple data model means no parameter-tweaking hard forks. BFT proof of stake built on a novel, collusion-proof game-theoretical model ensures immediate finality and resilient decentralization, eliminating the need to recover from attacks through forks. And future-proof cryptography allows Themelio to survive unforeseen cryptoanalytic breakthroughs.

Groundbreaking cryptocurrency

Themelio’s base currency, Mel, is the world’s first truly decentralized, fiat-independent stablecoin. Melmint, a fully trustless, oracle-free issuance mechanism, stabilizes a mel’s value against an index representing the time cost of sequential computation.

A decentralized stable-value asset completely demolishes the false dichotomy between trustless issuance and low volatility. With money that is independent of both the systemic risk of oracle-based fiat pegs and the extreme volatility of conventional cryptocurrencies, Themelio promises a fundamental breakthrough in decentralized financial applications.

Usable and performant

Themelio’s low-level UTXO-based data model is far from the limited, payment-focused models of blockchains like Bitcoin and Litecoin. MelVM, a Turing-incomplete yet expressive covenant language, empowers complex dApps with stronger security guarantees and higher performance than smart contracts in platforms based on mutable state, like Ethereum and Cardano. Usability is further enhanced by straightforward, easy-to-understand blockchain semantics. Themelio eliminates unintuitive corner-cases, like block reorganizations, common in other blockchains, makes it easy to build tall application stacks without frustration.

And despite Themelio being a traditional one-shard blockchain, apps using it can easily achieve great performance with its design that can secure layer-2 protocols without deploying full nodes. Even layer-1 usage benefits from a data model engineered for maximum performance through techniques like fully parallel transaction validation.
