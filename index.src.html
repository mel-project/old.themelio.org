What is Themelio?

Themelio is the low-level foundation for a new decentralized Internet. It's a blockchain designed the right way — like a core network protocol. Themelio is simple, future-proof, and ridiculously robust, just like the Internet Protocol.

Blockchains are broken.

Right now, decentralized app developers face terrible options.

You could spend years engineering and refining a multi-layer, specialized protocol just for your specific use case, like Satoshi Nakamoto did with Bitcoin and payments. You'd probably still make something insecure, hard to use, and impossible to evolve.

Or you could use a general-purpose blockchain like Ethereum or EOS. You'd just write the application layer, and let the Turing-complete smart contract support of the blockchain handle the rest of the stack.

But then come the poor performance, high costs, unintuitive behavior, and worst of all, disruptive and contentious updates. You'll probably end up wishing you could trust somebody else's computer after all.

A blockchain at the right layer.

When we made Themelio, we took an entirely different approach.

Unlike centralized services, blockchain protocols can't really be updated. A "hard fork" update is really the community switching to a new blockchain. And as with all collective action, contention, drama, and politics threaten the stability of the ecosystem.

So we didn't try to make Themelio the perfect blockchain for a single application, whose requirements might suddenly change. Neither did we create a deceptively easy-to-use smart contract virtual machine, papering over ever-evolving complexity with leaky abstractions.

Instead, Themelio is designed not to change. And that means keeping the protocol simple and the abstractions low-level.

Themelio focuses on being a layer-0 blockchain. Its only job: providing the fundamental properties of a blockchain — transparency, immutability, and decentralization — extremely robustly and efficiently.

Rock-solid robustness.

Themelio's protocol is carefully engineered for stability and robustness. A simple data model means no parameter-tweaking hard forks. BFT proof of stake ensures immediate finality and resilient decentralization. And future-proof cryptography allows Themelio to survive unforeseen cryptoanalytic breakthroughs.

Simple usability.

MelScript allows you to make complex dApps unprecedented on coin-based systems, innovative cryptocurrency mechanisms stabilizes coin prices and transaction fees, and easy-to-understand semantics eliminates unintuitive corner-cases common in other blockchains. Themelio makes it easy to build tall application stacks without frustration.

Scalable performance.

Despite Themelio being a traditional, linear blockchain, apps using it can easily achieve great performance. Thousands of on-chain transactions can be processed every second, fully secure yet incredibly thin clients let embedded devices trustlessly access the blockchain, and a design optimized for securing off-chain apps like payment channels enables massively scalable systems with blockchain-backed security.

The future is almost here.

Themelio is still under heavy development. It isn't ready for public use just yet.

There are several things you can do right now though. The whitepaper is available if you want a detailed look at Themelio's design. You can also read the work-in-progress knowledge base, which includes everything from technical documentation to developer tutorials. Our published research is also available.

If you're truly adventurous, follow the development of our prototype network. It's not feature-complete yet and it will break.

Read more about our plans for Themelio on the roadmap, or subscribe to updates at the bottom of the page.
