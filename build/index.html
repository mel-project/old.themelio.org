About Themelio

Blockchains are meant to be stable. Today’s solutions, however, remain volatile — threatened by governance models that can re-architect and upheave the foundations on which applications and ecosystems are built. Themelio is pioneering a new way to think about blockchain technology. Above all else, Themelio is designed to remain constant. It supports a new blockchain paradigm by providing a low-level, stable, and simple ‘Root of Trust’ that cannot be changed through governance processes. Immutability is the highest form of security and Themelio’s novel approach ushers in the revolution of trust that blockchains were always meant to bring.

Introducing Melmint

Melmint is the first cryptocurrency issuance system that enables a trustless stable cryptocurrency. MEL is the native cryptocurrency of Themelio — a stablecoin that isn’t pegged to any government-backed currency. MEL provides purchasing power for in-protocol fees and rewards, while the unpegged SYM token enables MEL to retain its peg and powers Themelio’s proof-of-stake architecture.

Immutability

Elegant in its simplicity, Themelio does not change. It decouples the blockchain from user-facing applications to prevent friction.

Minimal Governance

By eliminating governance from the core blockchain protocol, Themelio provides a rock-solid foundation that preserves blockchain’s core value: trust.

Vertical Scalability

Simple yet robust, Themelio’s highly-composable infrastructure allows a new ecosystem of untold innovations and evolutions to thrive.
